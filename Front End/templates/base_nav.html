  • Instructions to navigate our website:

    On this website, you can search through 241 ice creams from four different brands: Ben & Jerry's, Häagen-Dazs, Talenti, and Breyers. If you go to "Search in Categories" on the navigation bar, you can search ice creams in multiple ways:

    • Brand Search - which enables you to search ice creams by brands
    • Rating Search - which enables you to search ice creams based on the reviews/stars
    • Top Rated Search - which enables you to see the top x ice creams

    Aside from these features, you can also search ice creams by keywords. For example, if you search with "chocolate", you are prompted to the page with a list of ice creams whose name include the keyword "chocolate" (case insensitive).

    If you don't know where to start, go to "Suggest Me Ice Cream" on the navigation bar and click the button, which will give a randomly picked ice cream! Enjoy!
